Our Firm

Principled. Precise. Relentless.

LKR & Associates is a distinguished legal practice based in Bengaluru, founded on the principles of rigorous legal craftsmanship, unwavering client commitment, and ethical advocacy.

We represent individuals, businesses, and institutions across a broad spectrum of legal matters — bringing to every brief the same depth of analysis and dedication to outcome that defines our practice.

Led by Adv. Lalith Kumar R, the firm combines sharp legal acumen with a client-first philosophy, ensuring every matter receives the attention and expertise it deserves.

Our Philosophy

Every client deserves the highest standard of legal representation. We approach each matter as if it were the only matter — with undivided focus and strategic clarity.

Our Commitment

We maintain transparent communication throughout the legal process, ensuring clients are informed, empowered, and never left in uncertainty.

Our Reach

Practising before the High Court of Karnataka, District Courts, and Tribunals across Bengaluru and the surrounding jurisdiction.
